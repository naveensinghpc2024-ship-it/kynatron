About Kynatron Technologies

Engineering dependable industrial monitoring solutions for modern operations.

Our Story

Kynatron Technologies was founded by engineers who spent years working inside factories and saw a recurring problem — expensive, unreliable data loggers that couldn't withstand the Indian industrial environment. Too many machines failing in heat, dust, and power fluctuations.

We set out to build something better: rugged, accurate, and affordable data acquisition systems designed for the realities of a factory floor — not a laboratory. Our first product, the GM-DL Pro, was born from this conviction.

Today, Kynatron designs, develops, and manufactures a complete ecosystem — from multi-channel data loggers and wireless sensors to full SCADA monitoring platforms with cloud connectivity. Every product is engineered in-house at our facility in Hyderabad, India.
