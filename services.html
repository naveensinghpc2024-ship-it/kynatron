What We Offer

Data Acquisition & Logging

Precision measurement and recording for process-critical environments.

  • Multi-channel temperature monitoring
  • Custom sensor integration (TC, RTD, 4-20mA)
  • High-speed sampling up to 1 sec intervals
  • CFR 21 Part 11 compliant data formats
  • 50+ year retention without data overwrite

Industrial Monitoring Solutions

Real-time visibility into your plant floor, 24/7.

  • Live sensor dashboards (web-based, zero install)
  • Multi-zone temperature & humidity monitoring
  • Trend analysis and historical data viewing
  • Remote monitoring via WiFi/4G/Cloud
  • Custom KPI dashboards for management

Alarm & Control Systems
